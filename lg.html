About Me

Get to know my professional background and academic journey

Professor of Organic Chemistry

I am a dedicated academic professional with extensive experience in organic chemistry and pharmaceutical sciences. Currently serving as a Full Professor at Hawassa University, I have committed my career to advancing chemical knowledge and its applications in drug discovery and development.

My research focuses on computer-aided drug design, phytochemical investigation of medicinal plants, and development of environmentally friendly chemical processes.

Research Focus Areas

My primary research interests and specialized domains

Computer-Aided Drug Design

Development of computational models for designing potential inhibitors of enzymes like P. falciparum DHFR for antimalarial applications.

Phytochemistry

Isolation and characterization of bioactive compounds from Ethiopian medicinal plants with potential therapeutic applications.

Medicinal Chemistry

Synthesis and evaluation of nitrogen-containing heterocycles as potential anti-TB and antimalarial agents.

Green Chemistry

Development of environmentally friendly chemical processes including soap production from waste materials.

Natural Products

Exploration of Ethiopian flora for biologically active compounds with medicinal properties.

Food Chemistry

Analysis of caffeine content in Ethiopian coffee varieties using advanced analytical techniques.
